Costly Choices

Everyone says, "do whatever you want"
they don't mention the cost of it tho.
You dont know the price until,
your choices come close and tell you what you owe.
In moments you don't realize
consequences of what you do
but only after it's too late,
you can see what would have been best for you.
Some decisions are too expensive,
Until you get the bill you won't know.
By then you can't go back and choose different directions to go.
So bear in my mind that every action and mistake is a tattoo.
No matter how costly our regrets,
every one we can never undo.

The Celestial Serenity

We are like the sun and the moon,
the sun dies every night for the moon,
and the moon brings it back to life every morning.


Abandon

Why did you abandon me?
in time when I was in need,
where all but my liberty
was taken indeed.
Why must you abandon me?
as you left all the worries,
now I'm all alone,
hoping for the apologies.


Beyond the Cover

Humans treat each other like books,
If the cover doesn't catch their eye,
They won't even bother to read whats inside.
Ps: The cover is not the book, The body is not the person
